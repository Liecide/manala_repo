How to play

  • Point & click: click a tile or target to walk there. Diagonal movement is now supported.
  • Camera: right-drag to pan, wheel to zoom, arrow keys also pan.
  • Fishing: 3–5 second action with fish / junk / nothing outcomes.
  • Mining: 2–3 second action with ore / nothing outcomes.
  • Combat: simultaneous 3-hit exchange, then 5 seconds of immunity.
  • Dialogue: bank, forge, anvil, healer and merchant use proper windows.
  • Death: inventory and equipped gear drop on death; banked items stay safe.
