Manala

v7 local prototype — timed fishing + pseudo-isometric pass

How to play

  • Point & click: left-click ground to move, left-click trees/NPCs/enemies/stations to walk over and interact.
  • Fishing: takes 3–5 seconds and can reward fish, nothing, or junk.
  • Keyboard movement: A / D or Left / Right still works.
  • Jump: W, Up, or Space.
  • Interact manually: E near an object, enemy, drop, or station.
  • Eat food: H.
  • Equip inventory slots: 1–9.
  • Save / Load: buttons in the right panel.

All skills start at level 1. Combat uses a simultaneous 3-hit exchange, then a 5 second immunity window so you can heal or run before re-engaging.

Manala

Local RSC-style prototype — v7

Instructions

  • Left-click ground to move to a tile.
  • Left-click an NPC, bank, forge, tree, ore rock, fish spot, or enemy to path to it and interact.
  • Forge now supports Cook All and Smelt All batch actions at 1 item per second.
  • Combat: both sides trade a simultaneous 3-hit cycle, then both are immune for 5 seconds.
  • While in combat, non-combat interactions are locked so you cannot chop a tree during a wolf slap-fest like a possessed forklift.
  • The bank now shows a proper vault grid with item icons and withdraw buttons.
  • Death drops inventory, keeps banked items safe, and respawns you in the first village.
